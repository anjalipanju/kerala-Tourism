IDUKKI

Idukki is Kerala's second largest district and has been blessed with numerous natural wonders. Mist laden hills, enchanting valleys, gushing streams, cascades, rolling grasslands, sprawling plantations, colonial bungalows, wildlife and many more makes Idukki, a landlocked district in Central Kerala a unique destination for travellers. The hilly terrain, which has the highest point in South India- Anaimudi offers ample scope for trekking, other adventures sports. People love going to the gigantic arch dam for getting its mesmerising view. It is one of the districts in Kerala with a predominant tribal population. With the famous hill station Munnar as its crowning glory, each of them offers a spectacular view of the valley below and ranges above of Idukki.
